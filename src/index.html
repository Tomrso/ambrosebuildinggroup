Frequently Asked Questions

This usually includes cleaning the area, filling any holes or cracks, sanding rough patches and applying primer if needed. Proper preparation ensures the new paint adheres well and delivers a smooth, long-lasting finish. Contact our team if you'd like a professional inspection before repainting.

  • The cost of repainting an interior depends on the size of the home, the number of rooms, the condition of the surfaces and the level of preparation required. Homes needing repairs, patching or extensive preparation may require additional work. We provide a detailed inspection and quote so you know exactly what is involved before the project begins.

  • Exterior painting costs vary depending on the size of the property, the materials being painted (such as brick, render or weatherboard), access requirements and the condition of the existing paintwork. Preparation, repairs and the finish you choose can also affect pricing. We provide a free inspection and detailed quote to give an accurate estimate for your home.

  • Priming is essential in many situations, including painting new surfaces, covering stains, switching to drastically different colours or painting porous materials. Primer helps paint adhere properly and improves the durability and finish of the final coat. Skipping this step can lead to uneven coverage or premature peeling.

  • Interior paint typically lasts around 5 to 10 years depending on the quality of the paint used and how much wear the space receives. Exterior paint usually lasts around 5 to 7 years, although factors such as sun exposure, rain, coastal salt and general weather conditions can shorten this timeframe.

  • Different finishes are designed for different surfaces. Flat paint is commonly used for ceilings because it hides imperfections well. Matt or low sheen finishes are typically used on walls as they provide durability and are easier to clean. Semi-gloss or gloss finishes are usually used for trims, doors and woodwork because they are highly durable and very easy to wipe down.
