Is your backyard missing something?

A Portable BBQ Isn't an Outdoor Kitchen

Most Melbourne backyards have the space to entertain properly but nothing built to support it. A fold-out table and a freestanding BBQ work fine up to a point, but they never quite feel like a space worth spending time in.

When there's nowhere to prep food, store equipment or sit comfortably while cooking, the outdoor area gets underused — even on good weather days.

A properly built outdoor kitchen changes how the whole backyard functions. It becomes a natural place to cook, eat and entertain rather than an extension you only use when you have to.

We design and build outdoor kitchens around how you actually want to use your backyard, so the space works properly from day one.

Custom outdoor kitchens Melbourne

A Kitchen That Brings the Entertaining Outside

A well-built outdoor kitchen turns the backyard into a proper entertaining space. Cooking, dining and hosting all happen in the one area — and the indoor kitchen stays clean while the food gets done outside.

We build outdoor kitchens with built-in BBQs, stone or concrete benchtops, weather-rated cabinetry and sink setups where required. Everything is designed to suit the layout of your outdoor space and complement the style of the home.

An outdoor kitchen can also be combined with a deck, pergola or covered entertaining area as part of the same build. That way the whole outdoor space is designed to work together, with shelter overhead and a solid surface underfoot.

Every project starts with a site visit so the design suits your property, your style and how you want to use the space.
