Time for a new coat of paint?

When It’s More Than Just “A Bit Of Touch Up”

Some jobs are too big for patchwork fixes. Renovations, extensions and older homes often need a full repaint to make everything match and look right.

You might have new plaster next to old walls, fresh joinery that needs finishing, or different colours and paint lines from past work. Even when the build is done, the paint can be what makes the whole place feel complete.

A proper repaint is also common when preparing a home for sale, after moving in, or when the interior and exterior have been done at different times and no longer match.

Renovation painting works best when it’s planned properly and finished cleanly, so the whole home looks consistent.

Melbourne Painters

Renovation Painting Done Properly

We specialise in larger repainting projects where quality, planning and follow-through matter. The goal is to complete the full job properly so everything ties together and the finish looks sharp throughout the home.

We start with an inspection to understand what’s changing in the space, what needs painting, and what needs prep. Renovation jobs often involve a mix of old and new surfaces, which means the prep work and paint systems need to be done right.

Prep is where renovation painting is won or lost. New plaster, patched areas, repaired timber and existing paint all behave differently. We make sure surfaces are ready before any top coats go on, so the finish looks even and lasts. We handle repainting for:

  • Full home repaints
  • Renovations and extensions
  • New builds and fit-outs
  • Property refreshes before sale or lease
  • Builder handover painting
