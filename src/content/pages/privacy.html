Privacy

Privacy Policy

This website may collect and use limited personal information in order to respond to enquiries and improve how the site operates. We respect your privacy and aim to handle any information responsibly.

Information Submitted Through Contact Forms

When you submit a form on this website, we collect the information you provide. This may include your name, phone number, email address and the details of your enquiry.

This information is used only for the purpose of responding to your enquiry, providing quotes, arranging services, or communicating with you about the work you have requested.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. Your information is only accessed by the business for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and delivering services.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to help understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, device type and general geographic location.

This data helps us improve the website, understand which pages are useful to visitors, and improve the overall user experience. The information collected through Google Analytics does not personally identify individual users.

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect this information. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer not to be tracked.

Data Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect the information submitted through this website. However, no method of transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please contact the business using the contact details listed on this website.
