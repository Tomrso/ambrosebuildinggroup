When Your Home No Longer Feels Right

Many homes reach a point where the interior no longer suits the way people live.

Kitchens may feel outdated or too small for entertaining. Bathrooms can feel tired and cramped. Storage becomes limited and the layout of the home just doesn't work anymore.

Over time these small frustrations add up and the house starts to feel less comfortable than it should.

A well planned renovation can completely change how the home feels. Updated layouts, modern finishes and improved functionality can transform the space without needing to move house.

We work with homeowners across Melbourne’s Eastern Suburbs to redesign and rebuild interiors so the home works better for everyday living.

Asked questions

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Renovation costs depend on the size of the project, the materials selected and the level of structural work required. We provide a site inspection and detailed quote based on your renovation plans.

  • Some renovations may require permits depending on the scope of work and structural changes involved. We can advise on approvals during the planning stage of the project.

  • Timeframes depend on the size and complexity of the renovation. Smaller projects like bathrooms may take a few weeks, while full home renovations will take longer depending on the scope of work.

  • In some cases homeowners can remain in the property depending on the areas being renovated. Larger projects may require temporary relocation while construction is underway.

  • Ambrose Building Group works across Melbourne’s Eastern Suburbs including Mitcham, Blackburn, Ringwood, Doncaster, Camberwell and surrounding areas.
