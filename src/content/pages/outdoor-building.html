Outdoor Entertainment Building Experts

Deck Building

A custom deck creates the foundation of your outdoor living space. It’s where outdoor dining, relaxing and entertaining naturally happens, turning the backyard into somewhere people actually want to spend time.

Ambrose Building Group builds timber and composite decks designed to connect seamlessly with the home and create a comfortable, durable area for entertaining friends and family.

Pergola Construction

Pergolas add shade, structure and style to outdoor spaces, making them far more comfortable to use throughout the year. They help turn open backyards into defined entertaining areas that feel like a natural extension of the home.

Whether attached to the house or built as a freestanding feature, a well designed pergola creates a space where people can relax, gather and enjoy the outdoors.

Outdoor Kitchens

Outdoor kitchens take entertaining to another level by bringing cooking and socialising into the same space. Instead of running between inside and outside, everything happens in one comfortable outdoor area.

From built-in BBQs to complete outdoor cooking zones, these spaces are designed to make hosting friends and family easy.
