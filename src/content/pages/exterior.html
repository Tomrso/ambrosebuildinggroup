Time for a fresh exterior?

When Exterior Paint Starts To Break Down

Exterior paint takes a constant beating from Melbourne’s weather. Sun exposure, rain, wind and temperature changes slowly wear the surface down over time.

Many homes eventually reach a point where the outside begins to look faded, worn or just old.

You might start noticing things like peeling paint, chalky surfaces, exposed timber or colour fading on walls and trims.

Exterior painting doesn't just give your home a make over. It helps protect the building materials underneath from moisture, UV damage and long-term deterioration.

Professional Exterior Painting Done Properly

Exterior painting needs to be done carefully to last. We focus on complete exterior painting projects across Melbourne rather than quick touch-ups so the whole home looks consistent and protected.

Every job starts with a proper inspection of the property. Surfaces are checked for peeling paint, damaged timber, cracks or areas where preparation will be needed before painting begins.

Preparation is the most important part of exterior painting. Surfaces are cleaned, loose paint is removed and areas are repaired so the new paint bonds properly. We work on:

  • Exterior walls
  • Weatherboards
  • Render and brick surfaces
  • Window frames
  • Doors and trims
  • Eaves and fascias

The goal is to leave the home with a clean, consistent finish that looks great and protects the exterior surfaces for years.
